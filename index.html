CUE FRISTRATIONS!

Fristrations is the most efficient way to alleviate the search for available study spaces near you. Synthesizing data daily from the registrar and Frist's Room Reservation System, you can find empty classrooms nearby. You can also book rooms in real time in just a couple clicks.

How it works

Fristrations is an iOS application with Firebase as the backend. We scrape the registrar's page for the classes that take place in Frist and display the number of devices connected to router's nearby to let you know whether there are people around. You can book up to four time slots a day, favorite the rooms that you like the most, and check the available rooms at any given time.

  • Data

    Room availability information will be gathered by scraping the registrar's website for Frist classroom availability. We also plan on using access points and the number of devices connected to them to determine availability in Frist and Dillon Gym.

  • Requirements

    iOS 7+

    Princeton netID and password required and verified using CAS Authentication.

  • Security

    We are ensuring the safety of the users of this application by allowing only those with a valid Princeton netID to use the application. Users must authenticate themselves through the CAS in order to access the information.

  • Beyond the Course

    We plan on releasing this application to all members of the Princeton community after beta testing is completed.
