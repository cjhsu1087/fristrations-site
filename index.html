CUE FRISTRATIONS!

Fristrations is the most efficient way to alleviate the search for available study spaces near you. Synthesizing data daily from the registrar and Frist's wireless internet routers, Fristrations empowers you to find empty classrooms in Frist easily. You can also book rooms in real time in just a couple clicks.

How it works

Fristrations is an iOS application with Firebase as the backend. We scrape the registrar's page for the classes that take place in Frist and display the number of devices connected to router's nearby to let you know whether there are people around. You can book up to four time slots a day, favorite the rooms that you like the most, and check the available rooms at any given time.

  • Data

    Room availability information is gathered by scraping the registrar's website for Frist classroom availability. A real time representation of the number of devices connected to routers gives added insight into how filled a certain location is.

  • Requirements

    iOS 7+

    Princeton netID and password required and verified using CAS Authentication.

  • Security

    We are ensuring the safety of the users of this application by allowing only those with a valid Princeton netID to use the application. Users must authenticate themselves through the CAS in order to access the information.

  • Future Plans

    We are working with the Frist administration to make the data on room availability even more accurate. Further, we plan to expand services to other areas of campus as the app gains in usage by the student body.
